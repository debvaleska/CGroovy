SHORT DESCRIPTION

Ahead of their highly-anticipated second studio album, BORN PINK, BLACKPINK is enchanting with their charisma and talents on its pre-release single, “Pink Venom.”
The song kicks off with a classic hip-pop beat, with the melody being accompanied by traditional Korean instruments—including a gayageum, a pluck zither containing 12 strings. Additionally, the song's lyrics explore the girls' evil plan of attracting others into their dangerous-yet-lovely “Pink Venom.”
The music video is YG Entertainment's highest-budget music video, which showcases the girls in multiple dark scenes with an antique-yet-mystical feel.
